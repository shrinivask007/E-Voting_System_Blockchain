TrustVote :E-Voting using Blockchain

Our blockchain voting system revolutionizes the way votes are cast and counted. Leveraging cutting-edge technologies such as Ganache, Web3.js, MetaMask, Ethereum, and Truffle, we provide a voting platform that is secure, transparent, and reliable.

Why Blockchain Voting?

Blockchain technology offers a transparent and tamper-proof system for recording votes, ensuring that each vote is counted accurately and securely. Our platform leverages the power of blockchain to provide a fair and reliable voting experience.

Key Features

Security

Votes are stored on a decentralized blockchain, making it nearly impossible to tamper with or alter the voting records.

Transparency

Blockchain ensures that the entire voting process is transparent, allowing voters to verify that their votes were counted.

Reliability

With blockchain, we eliminate the possibility of voting fraud and manipulation, ensuring reliable election results.

Technology Stack

  • Ganache: Used for setting up a local blockchain network for testing and development.
  • MetaMask: A browser extension that allows users to interact with the Ethereum blockchain directly from their browsers.
  • Web3.js: A JavaScript library used to interact with the Ethereum blockchain.
  • Truffle: A development framework for Ethereum that provides tools for compiling and deploying smart contracts.

How It Works

Our platform uses a smart contract deployed on the Ethereum blockchain to handle the voting process. Voters use MetaMask to interact with the contract, ensuring that all votes are securely recorded on the blockchain. The system is transparent, allowing voters to verify that their vote was counted correctly without revealing their identity.
